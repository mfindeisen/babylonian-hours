What is this?


This clock shows you your local time computed using a Babylonian system of reckoning.
The Babylonians divided the day into four parts defined by the sunrise and sunset. I've used those definitions here instead of AM/PM. They are:
  • ASR: after sunrise
  • BST: before sunset
  • AST: after sunset
  • BSR: before sunrise
